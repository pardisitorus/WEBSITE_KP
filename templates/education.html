Why These Actions Matter

{% if risk_level == 'high' %}

Areas with high fire risk require immediate and comprehensive preventive measures. Forest fires in these locations can spread rapidly due to dry conditions and fuel availability, potentially causing significant environmental and economic damage.

{% elif risk_level == 'medium' %}

Medium-risk areas need regular monitoring and maintenance. While the immediate danger is lower, these areas can quickly become high-risk if preventive measures are neglected.

{% else %}

Low-risk areas should still maintain basic preventive practices. Even in safer areas, vigilance and community awareness are important for overall forest fire prevention.

{% endif %}
